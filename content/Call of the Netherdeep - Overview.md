This adventure takes place in the world of Exandria a land of epic heroes, ancient mysteries, and a more modern twist on the classic fantasy. In cities you may find technology, such as gondolas, rail cars, and airships brought aloft through the magic that dwells within the lands of Exandria.

This campaign begins in the goblin and orc town of Jigow although you will travel throughout the lands making your way to the desert metropolis of Ank'Harel and other such lands. Along the way you will find traces of a cosmic mystery and hear the plea of a warrior from ages long since past.
