[History of Exandria](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DYBM3myR914)

This adventure takes place in the world of Exandria a land of epic heroes, ancient mysteries, and a more modern twist on the classic fantasy. In cities you may find technology, such as gondolas, rail cars, and airships brought aloft through the magic that dwells within the lands of Exandria.

This campaign focuses on uncovering a cosmic mystery of sorts whilst journeying through varied landscapes from the desert metropolis of Ank'Harel to the town of Jigow full of orcs, goblins and the drow of the Kryn dynasty.
